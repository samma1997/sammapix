## SUBJECT LINE FORMULAS BY CATEGORY

### 1. EDUCATIONAL (High Open Rate: 18-22%)

**Pattern:** "[Number] [Benefit/Action] to [Outcome]"

Examples:
- "3 tools that'll save you hours this week"
- "5 ways photographers cut editing time by 80%"
- "4 image formats that destroy your site speed (and how to fix them)"

**Pattern:** "The [Noun] nobody's using (but should)"

Examples:
- "The image upscaler nobody's using (but should)"
- "The metadata stripper nobody knows about"

**Pattern:** "Did you know [Platform] can [Action]?"

Examples:
- "Did you know SammaPix can optimize PDFs too?"
- "Did you know you can batch watermark 100 images at once?"

## TESTING SUBJECTS — A/B TEST FRAMEWORK

### Test Format
Send to 50% of audience: Subject A
Send to 50% of audience: Subject B
Winner goes to main send next time

### Subject A vs B Examples

**Test 1: Curiosity vs Direct**
- A: "Did you know SammaPix can bulk watermark?"
- B: "Bulk Watermark Tool: Protect 100 images at once"
- (Curiosity usually wins: ~3-5% higher open rate)

**Test 2: Urgency vs Benefit**
- A: "24 hours left: 60% off"
- B: "Save 10 hours/week with Pro"
- (Urgency usually wins with free users, benefit with Pro users)

**Test 3: Personalization vs Broadcast**
- A: "[Name], you just hit 500 images 🎯"
- B: "Milestone: 500 images processed (real numbers inside)"
- (Personalization usually wins: ~5-7% higher open rate)

**Test 4: Question vs Statement**
- A: "Is SammaPix worth upgrading to?"
- B: "Here's exactly how much time SammaPix saves you"
- (Questions usually win: ~2-4% higher open rate)

## SUBJECT LINE LENGTH GUIDELINES

**Mobile (Most important — ~60% of opens):**
- Optimal: 35-50 characters
- Maximum before cutoff: 50 characters
- Example: "3 tools that'll save you hours" (30 chars) ✅

**Desktop:**
- Optimal: 50-70 characters
- Maximum before wrapping: 100 characters
- Example: "Pro photographers just got 10x faster uploads — here's why" (60 chars) ✅

**Test Both:** Always test a short (35 chars) vs medium (50-70 chars) version.

## EMOJI USAGE GUIDE

**Use Emoji When:**
- ✅ Milestone emails ("You've reached 1,000 images! 🎯")
- ✅ Celebration tone ("Welcome to Pro! 🚀")
- ✅ Important alerts ("Payment failed 🚨")
- ✅ Highest open rate boost (+2-5% typically)

**Avoid Emoji When:**
- ❌ Professional B2B tone
- ❌ Transactional/legal emails
- ❌ Multiple emojis in one subject (max 1-2)

**Best Performing Emojis for SammaPix:**
- 🎯 (Milestone/goal achievement)
- 🚀 (Launch/upgrade)
- 🎉 (Celebration)
- ✨ (New/premium)
- 💡 (Insight/tip)
- 🔥 (Hot/trending)
- 👋 (Goodbye/return)

## DO's AND DON'Ts FOR SUBJECT LINES

### DO's
✅ Use specific numbers ("Save 10 hours" not "Save time")
✅ Lead with benefit ("Photographers just got..." not "Update notification")
✅ Keep it short (35-50 chars ideal)
✅ Use power words: save, get, discover, unlock, join, boost, cut
✅ Test different lengths and styles
✅ Personalize when possible ([Name], you...)
✅ Create curiosity (questions, surprising stats)

### DON'Ts
❌ ALL CAPS (looks like spam)
❌ Multiple !!!! or ???? (spammy)
❌ Fake urgency ("Act now or regret it forever")
❌ Clickbait that doesn't match email
❌ Generic ("Check this out" or "Quick update")
❌ Jargon without benefit
❌ Repetitive subject lines week after week
