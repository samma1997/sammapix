## ❌ What You CANNOT Do

- Post to Facebook Groups (API removed April 2024)
- Submit stories to Hacker News via API (read-only)
- Post to Quora officially (no API, legal risk)
- Publish YouTube Community posts (no API)
- Use Medium reliably (deprecated)
- Use Twitter/X free tier for real applications (500/month = 1/day)
