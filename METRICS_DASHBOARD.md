## RED FLAGS TO WATCH

### Traffic Red Flags
- [ ] Traffic is flat by Day 7 (adjust content angle)
- [ ] Blog traffic is 50%+ lower than Reddit (revisit SEO)
- [ ] Reddit posts getting 0-10 upvotes consistently (wrong subreddits/angles)
- [ ] No comments on any Reddit posts (tone is too salesy)

### Engagement Red Flags
- [ ] Comments are mostly negative/skeptical (address concerns directly)
- [ ] Posts are being removed by mods (not following subreddit rules)
- [ ] No one clicking through to tool (CTA is too subtle)
- [ ] High bounce rate on blog (content isn't matching headline)

### Outreach Red Flags
- [ ] Email open rate below 20% (subject lines need work)
- [ ] Response rate below 10% (too salesy, customize better)
- [ ] No features/mentions from 15+ outreaches (targeting wrong people)
- [ ] All responses are negative (product/positioning problem)

### If Red Flag Appears:
1. Stop and diagnose
2. Make ONE adjustment
3. Test for 3-5 posts/emails
4. Measure impact
5. Proceed or adjust further
