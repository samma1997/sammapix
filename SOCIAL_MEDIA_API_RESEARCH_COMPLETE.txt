KEY FINDINGS
================================================================================

PLATFORMS WITH APIS:
- 15 out of 22 platforms have official APIs
- 14 are completely FREE
- 5 have zero-friction setup (Bluesky, Dev.to, Telegram, Discord, Slack)

WHAT WORKS BEST:
1. Bluesky - Easiest API, decentralized, growing fast
2. Dev.to - Best for blog posts, technical audience
3. Telegram - Reliable, no rate limit concerns
4. Discord - Webhooks, internal/community posts
5. LinkedIn - Professional reach, free tier works

WHAT'S DEAD:
- Facebook Groups (API removed April 2024)
- Hacker News (read-only, no posting API)
- Quora (no official API, legal risk)
- YouTube Community (no API)
- Medium (deprecated, unsupported)
- Twitter/X Free Tier (500 posts/month = 1 per day, impractical)

FASTEST MVP:
- Platform: Dev.to + Bluesky + Telegram
- Setup time: 15 minutes
- Cost: $0
- Code: 50 lines maximum
- Reach: 20K-50K technical audience

MOST COMPLEX:
- Instagram (3-step container flow)
- TikTok (upload then publish separately)
- LinkedIn (OAuth token management)
- Twitter/X (rate limiting complexity)
- Facebook (various permission scopes)
