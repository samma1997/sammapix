## 5. BREAK-EVEN ANALYSIS

### Current Economics at €300/month Budget

**Assumption Scenario: Realistic (Day 15-30)**
```
Monthly clicks: 600 (at €0.50 CPC)
Sign-up rate: 2.5% (tool page, no optimization)
Monthly sign-ups: 15
Free-to-paid rate: 2% (no urgency, free tier is generous)
Paid conversions: 0.3 (15 × 0.02)
Monthly revenue per user: $9 (monthly plan)

Monthly Revenue from Google Ads:
= 0.3 customers × $9 × 0.885 (Stripe fee, EUR conversion)
= €2.38/month

Cost: €300/month
**Break-Even Multiple**: 300 ÷ 2.38 = 126x cost-to-revenue ratio ❌
**Assessment**: NOT PROFITABLE in month 1
```

### Path to Break-Even (6-Month Projection)

**Key Assumption**: Lifetime Value of 1 paid customer
```
LTV Calculation (Realistic Scenario):
- Monthly subscription: $9/mo (net €8.44)
- Free-to-paid rate: 2%
- Average retention: 8 months (12% monthly churn typical for tools)
- One-time cost to acquire: €25 (€300 ÷ 12 signups)

Lifetime Value (LTV) = Avg. Subscription × Retention × Net Rate
= $9 × 8 × 0.885 = €63.72 per paid customer

CAC (Cost to Acquire Customer): €25 (€300 budget ÷ 12 signups)
LTV:CAC Ratio: €63.72 : €25 = **2.55:1** ✅ (Acceptable, need >1:1)
```

### 6-Month Financial Projection

| Month | Clicks | Sign-Ups | Paid Conv. | Revenue | Cumulative Cost | Cumulative Revenue | Break-Even? |
|-------|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 (Mar) | 600 | 15 | 0.30 | €2.38 | €300 | €2.38 | ❌ -€297.62 |
| 2 (Apr) | 580 | 15 | 0.30 | €2.38 | €600 | €4.76 | ❌ -€595.24 |
| 3 (May) | 570 | 14 | 0.28 | €2.36 | €900 | €7.12 | ❌ -€892.88 |
| 4 (Jun) | 550 | 14 | 0.28 | €2.36 | €1,200 | €9.48 | ❌ -€1,190.52 |
| 5 (Jul) | 540 | 13 | 0.26 | €2.19 | €1,500 | €11.67 | ❌ -€1,488.33 |
| 6 (Aug) | 530 | 13 | 0.26 | €2.19 | €1,800 | €13.86 | ❌ -€1,786.14 |

**Problem**: Single-month revenue from one customer (€2.38) doesn't break even in month 1.
**Solution**: Calculate payback from **lifetime value**, not one-month revenue.

### Break-Even via Lifetime Value Payback

```
Cohort 1 (Month 1): 0.30 paid customers
LTV per customer: €63.72
Cohort 1 LTV: 0.30 × €63.72 = €19.12 (vs €300 cost = 6.4% payback month 1)

Cohort 2 (Month 2): 0.30 paid customers
Cumulative LTV: €19.12 + €19.12 = €38.24

...continuing pattern...

Break-even occurs when cumulative LTV ≥ cumulative cost
Break-even month: Month 16 (~€300 × 16 / €19.12)
```

**Revised Answer**: At current performance, **break-even happens in Month 16 (~5 months)** if:
1. You maintain €300/month spend
2. Conversion rate stays at 2.5% sign-up × 2% free-to-paid
3. Retention stays at 8 months average

## 7. IS GOOGLE ADS WORTH IT AT THIS BUDGET? (ROI Decision Framework)

### Current Math (Pessimistic)
```
Monthly spend: €300
Monthly paid conversions: 0.30
Cost per paid conversion: €1,000

Monthly recurring revenue: €2.38 (one-month billing)
Annual revenue per customer: €28.56 (assumes €8.44/month net × 3.39 months avg)
Annual profit per customer: €28.56 - €1,000 = -€971.44 ❌
```

**Conclusion**: Not worthwhile at current conversion assumptions.

### Break-Even Requirements

**To break even in Year 1**:
```
Need: Monthly Revenue ≥ Monthly Cost
€300 cost = ? revenue

At LTV:CAC of 1.5:1 (industry minimum):
Needed paid conversions = €300 ÷ (LTV of €150) = 2 per month
Required sign-up rate = 2 signups ÷ 600 clicks = 0.33% ✅ (feasible)
Required free-to-paid = 0.33% × free-to-paid = 0.33% ÷ 2% = 16.5% ❌ (impossible)

Alternative: Improve sign-up rate
Needed conversion chain: 3% sign-up × 2% free-to-paid = 0.06%
600 clicks × 0.06% = 0.36 conversions (still not enough)
```

### Is It Worth It? Decision Tree

```
START: €300/month Google Ads budget

Q1: Can you improve sign-up rate to 4%+ (2x current)?
  └─ NO? → REDUCE budget to €150/month (test only)
  └─ YES? → Continue to Q2

Q2: Can you improve free-to-paid to 3%+ (1.5x current)?
  └─ NO? → Focus on product + organic growth first
  └─ YES? → Continue to Q3

Q3: Does LTV:CAC exceed 2:1?
  └─ NO? → Revisit after 6 months with more data
  └─ YES? → SCALE (increase budget to €500-700/month)
```

### Honest Assessment

**Short answer**: Not worth €300/month RIGHT NOW, but has potential.

**Why**:
1. Conversion rate (0.04-0.06%) is too low relative to spend
2. Product page (/tools/compress) is not optimized for conversions
3. Free tier is generous but no urgency for paid upgrade
4. Need at least 3-4x improvement in conversion to break even

**How to make it worth it**:
1. **Create dedicated landing page** (expected +2-3x conversion)
2. **Add urgency to free tier** (e.g., daily limits, feature limits)
3. **Improve free-to-paid messaging** (show Pro value clearly)
4. **Start with €150/month** (€5/day) to test with lower risk

**Recommendation**: **REDUCE to €150/month** until you improve conversion rate to 0.15%+ (3-4x current).
